The True Story of the Internet- People Power

The Internet has changed society and a new breed of entrepreneurs is shaping the digital future. The communication was uprising all around us. New generation companies like YouTube, MySpace and Facebook, turned the web into a two way participatory and democratic medium controlled by no-one and shaped by everyone. It was termed as Web 2.0 time when the World Wide Web sites emphasized on user generated content and social networking. Towards the end of the episode, the Founder of Craigslist says: “The web is not just about making money, it’s about setting communication free.”

Music industry was not happy with the file sharing as they were not profiting from the business. Companies like Napster created a platform to share files and music without buying cd. Music industry filed a case against Napster and in 2001 Napster was banned. Music Company won the case. Although they encouraged apple to create iTunes music where songs were sold 99 cents apiece with copyright protection which made companies music labels happy.

Internet has revolutionized the 21st century. It is dominating and influencing man’s everyday life. With time, new inventions have been made, all trying to make life as convenient as possible. In other words, people are living smart now (with the introduction of smart wearables and gadgets, all connected by the web).

The spread of knowledge and information regarding various things is also credited to the web. Now any kind of information that a man needs is just a click away. Net has changed tremendously over the time and has also majorly influenced people. It still holds massive potential and one can never predict what new invention the future might unveil.
